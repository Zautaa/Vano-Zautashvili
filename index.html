Kakheti

The Kakheti Wine Region is located in the eastern part of Georgia and comprises two river basins, Iori and Alazani. These rivers have a significant influence on the character of Kakhetian wines.

    Popular Wines:
  • Saperavi - Red Dry
  • Rkatsiteli - White Dry

      • Popular Cities:
    • Telavi
    • Sighnaghi
    • Kvareli


Kartli

Kartli is one of the main wine regions in Georgia with a rich historical background. It is famous for white, red, and sparkling wines with a strong characteristic profile.

    Popular Wines:
  • Shavkapito - Red Dry
  • Goruli Mtsvane - White Dry

      • Popular Cities:
    • Gori
    • Bolnisi
    • Tskhinvali


Imereti

Imereti is a historical-geographical part of Georgia with very rich wine culture and heritage. It is famous for producing white wines. Also, most of the Georgian sparkling wines are produced in this wine region. Imereti terroir is excellent for producing high acid, fresh and vivid wines.

    Popular Wines:
  • Otskhanuri Sapere - Red Dry
  • Krakhuna - White Dry

      • Popular Cities:
    • Kutaisi
    • Chiatura
    • Sviri


Racha-Lechkhumi

Racha-Lechkhumi is one of the smallest Georgian wine regions. Unlike other wine regions, here vineyards are pretty small and scarce across the territory. However, wines from here are of high quality and very distinctive.

    Popular Wines:
  • Khvanchkara - Red Semi Sweet
  • Tvishi - White Semi Sweet

      • Popular Cities:
    • Oni
    • Tsageri
    • Ambrolauri


Black Sea Coast Area

On the Black Sea Coast, where vineyards are located on lower altitudes, it is divided into Adjara, Abkhazia, Guria, and Samegrelo wine regions. wines from these regions are more light-bodied, crisp, and refreshing.

    Popular Wines:
  • Chkhaveri - Red/Rose Dry
  • Chvitiluri - White Dry

      • Popular Cities:
    • Batumi
    • Sokhumi
    • Zugdidi
